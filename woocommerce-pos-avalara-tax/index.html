WooCommerce POS Avalara Tax Integration

Automate compliance and calculate exact sales tax instantly at your physical retail checkouts. Seamlessly connects Webkul WooCommerce POS with Avalara AvaTax.

Get StartedBuy Now

Why integrate Avalara AvaTax into your WooCommerce POS?

Bridge your physical store transactions and tax automation rules without complex manual lookups.

Outlet
Address-Based Taxation
Use physical WooCommerce POS outlet addresses as origin/destination addresses for precision local rate calculation.
Real-Time
Cart Tax Updates
Calculate exact sales tax dynamically on the POS frontend when adding items, changing quantities, or modifying carts.
Mapped
Virtual/Custom Items
Automatically maps custom items added on the fly by cashiers to temporary catalog products to ensure AvaTax compliance.
Flexible
Discount & Fee Calculations
Accurately recalibrates tax calculations when store discounts, coupons, or Stripe transaction fees are applied.
Seamless
Admin Controls
Easily toggle the Avalara tax computation flow off/on or switch to native WooCommerce tax tables per checkout.
Detailed
Tax Line Display
Display split tax lines matching local, county, state, and special district taxes clearly on POS receipts and invoices.
