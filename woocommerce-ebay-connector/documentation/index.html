Introduction – WooCommerce eBay Connector

The WooCommerce eBay Connector plugin integrates your WooCommerce store with eBay because managing them separately is enough to sap all of your time and drain profits.

eBay is a multinational e-commerce corporation that facilitates online consumer-to-consumer and business-to-consumer sales. It is one of the largest online marketplaces in the world — a leading website where users can buy and sell their products globally.

Overview

This eBay Connector plugin provides two-way integration, which means the admin can sync products from the WooCommerce site to eBay and vice versa.

The admin can:

  • Import information about products, categories, and orders from eBay to the WooCommerce store.
  • Import customized store categories from the eBay store to the WooCommerce site.
  • Export products from the WooCommerce store to the eBay store.
  • List items from the WooCommerce site directly onto your eBay account.
  • Provide eBay condition & specifications, refund policy, and shipping details for exported products.

  • Compatible with High-Performance Order Storage (HPOS)
