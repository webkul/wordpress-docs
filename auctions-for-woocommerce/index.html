Auctions for WooCommerce

Live bidding, anti-sniping, proxy bidding, customer wallet, multi-vendor support — the complete auction marketplace for WooCommerce.

Quick Start →View Features

Five-minute setup

Activate the plugin, walk the onboarding tour, and publish your first live auction before your coffee gets cold.

Ten auction types

Standard, reverse, sealed, proxy, silent, charity, penny, Dutch, seated, and unique-bid — all in one plugin.

Real-time bidding

Server-locked transactions, adaptive polling, anti-sniping soft-close, proxy auto-bid, and a hash-chain audit ledger.

Built-in wallet

Customers top up, withdraw, transfer, and pay auction fees from a unified wallet with full admin controls.

Multi-vendor ready

Plug-and-play with WCFM, Dokan, WC Vendors, and YITH. Vendor onboarding stays in your existing vendor plugin.

Premium guardrails

Fraud detection, compliance reporting, dispute resolution, two-factor login, and Cloudflare Turnstile / hCaptcha gates.
