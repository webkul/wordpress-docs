WooCommerce POS Documentation

Complete WooCommerce Point of Sale (POS) documentation for managing online and physical stores, inventory, outlets, agents, and offline sales.

DocumentationView DemoBuy Now

Inventory Management

Manage product stock in real-time across outlets and automatically sync inventory after every sale.

Hardware Support

Seamlessly connect barcode scanners, receipt printers, cash drawers, and other POS hardware.

Payment Terminal

Accept secure payments via integrated payment terminals supporting cards, UPI, and digital wallets.

Cash Drawer

Automatically trigger the connected cash drawer for cash-based transactions.

Multisite Compatibility

Manage multiple WooCommerce stores from a single POS dashboard.

Drawer Management

Monitor opening balance, cash in/out, and closing balance for each POS session.

Outlet Reporting

Generate detailed outlet-wise sales, revenue, and performance reports.

Hold Cart

Save carts temporarily and resume them later without losing product data.

WhatsApp Notification

Send order confirmations and updates to customers via WhatsApp.

Dynamic Theme

Customize the POS interface with dynamic themes to match your brand.

Cashier Management

Manage multiple cashiers and assign them to different outlets.

Custom Product

Add custom or ad-hoc products to the cart that are not listed on the website.
