License Management & Activation

Manage your purchases and activate your WooCommerce modules in just a few steps.

1. User Dashboard

Your dashboard is the central hub for all your license management activities.

Key Features:

  • Consolidated Purchases – View both Envato and Webkul Store purchases side-by-side.
  • License Status – Instantly identify active, pending, or expired licenses.
  • Domain Access – Update the domains linked to your software keys.

2. License Creation

Generate your license keys on-demand without manual approval delays.

Step 1 – Select Your Purchase

  • Locate your product in the purchase history.
  • Select either your Envato Purchase Code or Webkul Order ID to begin.

Step 2 – Configure & Generate Keys

  • Enter Domain – Provide your Domain name (e.g., example.com).
  • Environment Support – Create keys for both staging and production environments simultaneously.

Step 3 – View License List & Details

  • Retrieve the generated license key from the license list.
  • Check the full configuration details and support eligibility.

3. Module Activation

The final step is to activate the module in your WordPress admin panel.

Steps:

  • Navigate to Settings – Open the module's specific settings page in the WordPress admin panel.

  • Enter License Key – Paste the generated key into the license key field and click Validate.

Successful Activation – Your module is now fully functional!
