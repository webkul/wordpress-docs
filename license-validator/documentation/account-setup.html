Account Registration & Sign In

Follow these simple steps to register your purchase and access your dashboard.

You can access the License Portal here:
Login to License Portal

1. Registration

Register your purchase using either your Envato details or your Webkul Store credentials.

Registration via Envato

If you purchased from CodeCanyon (Envato), use your purchase code to link your account.

Note: If you don’t have your purchase code or don't know where to find it, follow this guide:
Where Is My Purchase Code?

Steps:

  1. Select Envato – Choose the Envato option on the registration page.

  2. Enter Purchase Details – Provide your Envato Username and the 36-character Purchase Code. Then, click on Verify User to validate your purchase.

  3. Enter Email – Once verified, provide an active email address for your account. and then input the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered email address to complete the registration.

(Note: The email address can only be updated once from your profile.)

Registration via Webkul Store

If you purchased directly from the Webkul Store, use your Order ID.

Steps:

  • Enter Order Information – Provide your Order ID and the email used during purchase.
  • OTP Verification – Enter the One-Time Password sent to your email to verify ownership.

2. Sign In (Email + OTP)

Once registered, you can log in anytime using your email. No password is required.

Steps:

  • Enter Email – Enter your registered email on the Sign In Page.

  • Receive OTP – A 6-digit verification code will be sent to your inbox.

  • Verify & Access – Enter the OTP to instantly access your dashboard.
