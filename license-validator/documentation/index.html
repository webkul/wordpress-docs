User Guide Overview

Welcome to the License Validator documentation. This guide will help you register your purchase, manage your licenses, and activate your module.

🚦 Your Journey

The process is designed to be quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Account Setup

    • Register your purchase (Envato or Webkul Store).
    • Sign in using Email + OTP.

  2. License Management & Activation

    • Access your User Dashboard.
    • Generate your License Keys.
    • Activate the module in your WordPress site.
